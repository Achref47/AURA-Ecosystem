# Document Purpose

This Program Increment (PI) serves as the master engineering roadmap governing the evolution of AURA Product 1 following the successful migration from the legacy Version 4 intelligence engine to the Version 17/19 runtime ecosystem.

Unlike previous engineering phases, this Program Increment does **not** focus on machine learning research or model development.

The intelligence platform has already been engineered.

The objective now is to expose, operationalize, polish, validate, and commercialize that intelligence through a world-class seller experience.

This document is the single source of truth for all remaining Product 1 engineering work until Freeze.

# Product Architecture Evolution

## Architectural Context

During the engineering verification phase of Product 1, a comprehensive architecture assessment was performed covering:

* the V17 production notebook,
* the exported V17/V19 artifact ecosystem,
* the Product 1 runtime,
* the final dataset architecture,
* the AURA Dataset Version Evolution,
* the Product 1 migration strategy.

This assessment confirmed that Product 1 has evolved beyond a traditional machine learning application.

Rather than operating as a single prediction pipeline, the platform now follows a layered intelligence architecture that separates predictive intelligence from contextual intelligence while preserving the integrity of the validated production models.

This architectural decision is formally governed by **ADR-009 – Dual-Plane Intelligence Architecture**.

---

# Dual-Plane Intelligence Architecture

Product 1 is built upon two independent architectural intelligence planes.

These planes are architecturally complementary while remaining operationally independent.

## Plane 1 — Predictive Intelligence

The Predictive Intelligence Plane is implemented by the validated V17 production runtime.

This plane is responsible for:

* property valuation
* fair value estimation
* confidence estimation
* uncertainty quantification
* liquidity prediction
* probability of sale
* expected time on market
* strategic recommendations
* explainability

The Predictive Intelligence Plane is the only subsystem allowed to generate model-derived decisions.

Its runtime contract is frozen.

Its feature contract is frozen.

Its inference behaviour must remain stable throughout the Product Evolution Program.

No contextual datasets may enter this prediction pipeline unless a future engineering program performs complete retraining and revalidation.

---

## Plane 2 — Contextual Intelligence

The Contextual Intelligence Plane is implemented using the final domain-separated dataset architecture.

Its responsibilities include:

* spatial intelligence
* municipality intelligence
* market intelligence
* tourism intelligence
* volatility intelligence
* historical analysis
* regional comparison
* market exploration
* dashboard analytics
* contextual decision support

The Contextual Intelligence Plane does not replace prediction.

Instead, it provides the surrounding intelligence required to understand and interpret predictive outputs.

---

# Runtime Separation

The two intelligence planes remain intentionally separated.

Prediction remains deterministic and artifact-driven.

Context remains descriptive and read-only.

Composition occurs only at the presentation and experience layer.

Semantic fusion is prohibited.

Prediction outputs never consume contextual datasets.

Contextual intelligence never modifies predictive outputs.

This preserves:

* scientific validity,
* runtime reproducibility,
* explainability,
* governance,
* leakage protection,
* long-term maintainability.

---

# Runtime Responsibilities

The Product 1 runtime therefore consists of two complementary runtime layers.

### Predictive Runtime

Powered exclusively by:

* V17/V19 artifact bundle
* validated production models
* reconstructed feature contract
* frozen inference pipeline

Produces:

* valuation
* fair value
* confidence
* uncertainty
* liquidity
* strategic decision
* explainability

### Context Runtime

Powered by validated runtime projections of the final platform datasets.

Provides:

* municipality intelligence
* market trends
* tourism indicators
* volatility indicators
* regional comparisons
* contextual dashboards

These services remain read-only and never contribute features to the prediction models.

---

# Architectural Principles

The Product Evolution Program shall follow the following architectural principles.

1. Predictive and contextual intelligence are separate bounded contexts.

2. Prediction outputs must never depend on contextual datasets without complete retraining and validation.

3. Contextual intelligence remains read-only within Product 1.

4. Heavy analytical datasets remain outside the Product 1 runtime.

5. Semantic fusion is prohibited.

6. Intelligence planes compose only at the presentation layer.

7. Every intelligence plane owns its own contracts and validation process.

8. Prediction contracts remain stable throughout Product Evolution.

9. The platform data layer remains the authoritative source of contextual intelligence.

10. Product 1 consumes validated runtime projections rather than raw platform datasets.

11. Product 1 preserves scientific reproducibility while progressively exposing richer contextual intelligence.

---

# Sprint Alignment

This architecture directly governs the Product Evolution roadmap.

Sprint 0

Validate the Predictive Intelligence Plane and verify readiness of the Contextual Intelligence architecture.

Sprint 1–4

Expand and expose the complete Predictive Intelligence generated by the V17 runtime.

Sprint 5

Introduce the Contextual Intelligence Plane through dedicated backend services, APIs, dashboards, municipality exploration, market analytics, and contextual seller workflows.

Sprint 6

Unify both intelligence planes through an enterprise-grade seller experience while preserving architectural separation.

Sprint 7

Validate both planes independently and together before Product Freeze.

---

# Long-Term Platform Vision

The Product 1 evolution aligns with the long-term architecture of the AURA ecosystem.

Product 1 remains the authoritative predictive intelligence application.

The platform-wide contextual intelligence layer continues to evolve independently and will ultimately integrate with the broader AURA ecosystem through well-defined service boundaries.

This separation enables Product 1 to preserve its validated scientific models while benefiting from the continual evolution of the platform intelligence architecture.

## Architecture Decision Summary

Following Sprint 0 engineering verification, Product 1 adopts the
Dual-Plane Intelligence Architecture.

This decision preserves the validated V17 predictive runtime while
aligning Product 1 with the long-term AURA platform architecture.

The predictive runtime remains the sole producer of model-derived
decisions.

The contextual runtime provides market, spatial, tourism, and
volatility intelligence through independent services.

The two planes are composed only at the presentation layer and never
share prediction features.

This architecture is governed by ADR-009.
